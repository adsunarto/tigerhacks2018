About

Maestro allows you to easily transcribe, translate, and transform any article you want. Simply supply existing document or audio files to the drop area and watch them all load in where you can edit them directly.

Once satisfied you can move on to the translation, and summarization stage. Here you can make use of our hand-crafted summarization algorithm or use Google's top of the line translation service to make your document accessible in any language.
